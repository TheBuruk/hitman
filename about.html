Hitman: Contracts Routlette

This is a simple variation of Hitman: Blood Money roulette.

Metrics

  • Silent Assassin rating, Proffessional difficulty
  • Close Combat kills (Fiber Wire and melee can be distinguished by the given items in the level if asked.)
  • If the game asks you just for a CC kill then any CC kill is allowed. If it's specified, then the player has to obtain that item from the level.
  • Loud and silenced weaponries may be included.
  • If a specific waepon that do not exist in the level is asked, then the player can bring it through NG+ (NG+=completion after a new game.)
  • If no knock outs is asked then the player shouldn't try other means of KO'ing NPCs.
  • Heading start points may not vary too much, but every level has some kind of different way of routing (e.g. suit only or main enterances.)
  • If the player is asked to obtain an item, then that can be obtained until the end of the run.
  • Specific disguises for kills can be changed before and after objective completion.
